THE HORSEMEN

Praveen Kumar

“A fragile human outside.
A horse inside.”

Welcome to The Horsemen Journal

My name is Praveen Kumar.

For the past eleven years, I have lived among horses — not to control them, but to understand them.

Horsemanship, to me, is not about force or perfection.
It is about presence, patience, and quiet trust.

Horses do not respond to what you show on the outside.
They respond to what you carry within.
