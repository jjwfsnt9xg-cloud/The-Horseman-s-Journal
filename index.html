Praveen Kumar

“A fragile human outside.
A horse inside.”

The Black Stallion Who Whispered My Name in Hoofbeats

Welcome to The Horseman Journal
My name is Praveen Kumar.

For the last eleven years I have lived among horses — learning from them, working with them, and slowly letting them reshape me.

Horsemanship, to me, has never been about domination or force. It is about communication, patience, presence, and trust.

I have lived eleven years among horses, and still the world feels too small for me everywhere except in their shadow.

I am ordinary. Quiet. A heart that races — never quite falling below 120 — like a drum that forgot how to rest.

Shame has been my oldest companion.

Then the black stallion walked into my days.

I let go of perfection.
I let go of force.
I stood still. I breathed. I waited.

And slowly…

He began to believe.

Now he rests his head on my chest… and everything becomes quiet.

Eleven years. One horse. A heart that finally learned how to rest.
