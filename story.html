The Horsemen

Praveen Kumar

“A fragile human outside.
A horse inside.”

The Beginning

For eleven years I have been found again and again by beings who do not speak. I learned to stop shaping every moment and to instead hold still — to breathe, to watch, to wait.

The black stallion taught me that trust arrives only when force is removed; that softness, steadiness, and simple presence will coax life toward you when nothing else does.

This is a journal of those lessons — of movement, silence, and the truth horses reveal without ever speaking.

Horse Philosophy

Horses do not follow force.

They follow feeling.

They read what you cannot hide — your breath, your tension, your truth.

When you become calm, they come closer.
